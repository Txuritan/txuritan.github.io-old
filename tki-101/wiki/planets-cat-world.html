Cat World is very much like earth, in fact it is earth in an alternative univese where we are cat people. One of the most famous places in Cat World is Catland, a city located in the state of Maine. It is the home of the Martian Tutrok and his best friend Mayshin who is a cat. Cat World is run by AG Industries which is an alien research industry that studies and uses alien advance technology.
